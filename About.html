About

A song for who are creating sounds as beautiful and dramatic as the landscape that envelops them. From the Dingle Peninsula, the three creatives friendship gave way to a tuneful collaboration, borne out of a desire to write music. With songs reflecting both the isolation and consolation of Dingle, this year A Song For Who set to add to their increasing number of admirers.

Since their formation just over 12 months ago, A Song For Who have toured across the country with workmanlike dedication to their craft, developing their passion for playing live and with standout performances at many festivals in 2012. A Song For Who are set to return to many more festivals this summer including Sea Sessions, Castlepalooza and Indiependence.
